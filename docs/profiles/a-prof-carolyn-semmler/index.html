A/Prof Carolyn Semmler

by Deborah Apthorp, 11 Sep 2023



Associate Professor Carolyn Semmler leads the Applied Cognition and Experimental Psychology (ACEP) research group at the University of Adelaide, focusing on the application of theories and models of cognition, judgement and decision-making to legal and medical contexts. She and her group are interested in understanding human interaction with intelligent agents and the development of human-machine interfaces for defence and national security. Their work is driven by human rights and the value of reducing harm to humans through technology and innovation.
