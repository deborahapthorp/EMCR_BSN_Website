Dr Kristin Barry is an auditory neuroscientist at the University of Western Australia and Ear Science Institute Australia. Dr Barry’s expertise lies using electrophysiology, behavioural neuroscience, calcium imaging and translational animal models to investigate hearing loss, cognition, tinnitus, and neuronal excitability.
